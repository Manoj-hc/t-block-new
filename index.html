About Program

What is the T-block accelerator

The T-Block Accelerator is aimed at enabling startups that have a strong blockchain use case. This programme takes seed and validation-level startups with strong business and blockchain use cases and brings them to an investible stage. As a four-month-long accelerator program, T-block is aiming to support startups that are focused on building products and services that are revolutionising the deep tech industry. The Telangana Government and Tech Mahindra have partnered with IBC Media to access the Indian Tech startup ecosystem for the T-block accelerator program.

Selected start-ups will have an opportunity to pitch innovative ideas and get mentored by experts from the government of Telangana, Tech Mahindra, C-DAC and IIIT Hyderabad.
